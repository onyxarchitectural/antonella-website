About Me

I am a psychologist specializing in learning difficulties with a postgraduate degree in early stimulation. I work with children from 3 months to 9 years old, combining Montessori methodology with evidence-based child development frameworks.

My approach is always progressive, respecting each child's unique rhythm and empowering families as fundamental allies in the therapeutic process.
